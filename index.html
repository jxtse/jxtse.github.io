About Me

I’m a 3rd-year undergraduate student majoring in Information and Computing Science at Beijing Jiaotong University. I previously worked with Prof. Colin Tan and Prof. Boyd Anderson in the SOC Summer Workshop at the National University of Singapore. Now I am also a research intern in Wangxuan Institute of Computer Technology, Peking University.

As a dedicated student with a multidisciplinary background in Mathematics and Computer Science, I am keen on exploring opportunities that can deepen my understanding of Responsible AI, Large Language Models, and Natural Language Processing.

With strong foundations in research and leadership roles, I am actively seeking MPhil or Ph.D. programs where I can use my expertise for technological advancement. I am drawn to forward-thinking institutions where I can integrate AI responsibly into real-world applications, ensuring they are user-centric and ethically sound.
